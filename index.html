About me

A finance leader fluent in data, risk, and now AI.

I'm Scott Foster, a corporate finance and business intelligence leader based in Jacksonville, Florida, with an MBA in Finance & Management from Fordham University. My foundation is advanced financial modeling, SQL data warehousing, and the big-data platforms that turn raw information into decisions.
