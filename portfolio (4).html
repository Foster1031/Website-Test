About me

A designer who codes, and a developer who sweats the details.

I'm Scott — a product designer based in Lisbon with eight years spent moving between Figma and the browser. I care most about the moment a rough idea clicks into something people actually want to use.

My work lives in the overlap: design systems, accessible interfaces, and the front-end craft that keeps a polished mockup polished once it ships. When I'm not pushing pixels, I'm chasing the light with a film camera.
